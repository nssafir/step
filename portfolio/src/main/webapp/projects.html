What Have I Done

TNO Intern (Summer 2019) - I worked under Dr. Ernst-Jan Buis this summer at the Netherlands Organization for Applied Scientific Research (TNO). My task was creating an ML model to differentiate between high-frequency neutrino signals and underwater dolphin harmonics by analyzing hydrophone data from a large international particle physics experiment (KM3NeT). I developed a pipeline in Python using signal processing algorithms and convolutional neural network models and achieved over 99% accuracy in differentiating between dolphin clicks and simulated neutrino signals.
