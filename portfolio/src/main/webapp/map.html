Map of My Life

This map shows the places I have lived and the places I have visited. The larger, dark icons show places I have lived and the smaller, light icons show places I have visited. You can hover any of these icons to see the names of these places and when I visited or lived in them. For places I have lived, you can click on the icons to learn more about my experiences there.
